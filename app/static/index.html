How to file

  1. Confirm the APN above matches your property tax bill.
  2. Go to the Santa Clara County Assessor's online Prop 8 request: asr.santaclaracounty.gov/online-services.
  3. Enter the APN, your contact info, and the Opinion of Value above.
  4. Add the 3 comparable sales (APN/address, date, price) exactly as listed.
  5. Paste the comment into the “Other Information” box, then submit before the deadline (recommended by Aug 8).

This tool prepares a request from public data and comparable sales for your review. It does not submit anything on your behalf and is not legal or tax advice. Verify every figure before filing.
